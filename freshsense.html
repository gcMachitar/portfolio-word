About FreshSense

Mushrooms are delicate crops. A small change in their environment can cause mold, bad bacteria, or unwanted smells that ruin the entire harvest.

FreshSense is a smell-detection system that identifies early odors and alerts farmers long before contamination becomes visible.

It works like a “nose that never sleeps,” protecting farms 24/7.

Why Smell Matters

Most mushroom problems start quietly. Early odor detection gives farmers time to save their harvest.
